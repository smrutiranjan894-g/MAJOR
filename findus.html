FIND US

Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology, commonly known as VSSUT, is located in Burla, a small town in the Sambalpur district of Odisha, India. The university is well connected by both rail and road networks, making it easily accessible to visitors and students. If you're traveling by train, the nearest railway station to VSSUT is the Hirakud railway station. After Hirakud the nearest is Sambalpur railway station, which is approximately 15 kilometers away. From the railway station, you can either hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the university. Several buses are available from the Sambalpur bus stand, located adjacent to the railway station, that go directly to Burla. Alternatively, you can also take a train to Jharsuguda railway station or flight to the Jharsuguda Airport , which is approximately 60 kilometers away from VSSUT. From the railway station, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the university. Several buses are available from the Jharsuguda bus stand, located just outside the railway station, that go directly to Burla. If you're traveling by bus, the nearest bus stand to VSSUT is the Burla bus stand, which is located just 1 kilometer away from the university. From the bus stand, you can either walk to the university or take a rickshaw, which is a popular mode of transport in the town. Another option is to take a bus to the Sambalpur bus stand, located approximately 16 kilometers away from VSSUT. From the bus stand, you can either hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the university. In conclusion, VSSUT is well connected by both rail and road networks, making it easily accessible to visitors and students. If you're traveling to the university, you can either take a train to the Sambalpur or Jharsuguda railway station, or take a bus to the Burla or Sambalpur bus stand, and then hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the university.
